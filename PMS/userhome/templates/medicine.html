Common Poultry Medications

Amoxicillin

A broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections in poultry.

Colistin

Used to treat and prevent bacterial infections in poultry, particularly E. coli infections.

Enrofloxacin

A fluoroquinolone antibiotic used to treat respiratory and gastrointestinal infections.

Tylosin

Used for controlling chronic respiratory disease and infectious sinusitis in poultry.

Sulfaquinoxaline

Used to treat coccidiosis, a common parasitic disease in poultry.

Newcastle Vaccine

A vaccine used to prevent Newcastle disease, a highly contagious viral disease in poultry.
